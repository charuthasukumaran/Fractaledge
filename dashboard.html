FractalEdge — Overview
A multifractal analysis platform for Indian and global markets. It applies MFDFA (Multifractal Detrended Fluctuation Analysis) and MFDCCA (Multifractal Detrended Cross-Correlation Analysis) to detect market regimes, validate breakouts, and measure cross-asset coupling — going far beyond traditional technical indicators.

The Fractal Analysis Pipeline
1
Regime Detection via MFDFA
The Hurst exponent h(q=2) reveals the market regime: h > 0.5 = trend-persistent (breakouts sustain), h < 0.5 = mean-reverting (breakouts fail). The spectral width (Δα) indicates complexity — widening often precedes regime shifts.
2
Cross-Correlation via MFDCCA
Measures how a stock's fractal structure couples with its sector/index at different scales. Strong persistent cross-correlation = sector-driven breakout (reliable). Weak coupling = idiosyncratic move (higher risk/reward).
3
Scale-Validated Support/Resistance
S/R levels validated against dominant scaling regimes. A level aligning with where h(q) is highest is far more significant than single-timeframe levels.
4
Breakout Quality Scoring
Combines Hurst persistence, spectrum stability, MFDCCA alignment, S/R validation, and volume confirmation into a composite quality score (0-1). Filters out false breakouts.
5
Ensemble Signal & Regime Classification
All metrics feed into an ensemble score that classifies the market as GREEN (low stress), AMBER (elevated), or RED (high stress). Markov chain models predict regime transitions.
Smart Tools & Features
H
Market Health Score
Composite 0-100 score combining regime, Hurst alignment, coupling strength, RSI zone, and volatility into a single health grade (A+ to F).
R
Regime Prediction
Markov chain transition probabilities predict the next regime shift. Shows confidence, trend direction, and estimated bars until change.
T
AI Trade Plan
Claude AI generates a full trade plan with entry, targets, stop-loss, and position sizing based on current fractal data and regime.
P
Position Sizer
Calculate optimal position size based on account size, risk percentage, entry price, and stop-loss. Shows risk amount, max loss, and direction.
S
Portfolio Stress Test
Aggregate fractal analysis across portfolio holdings. Weighted stress scores, regime distribution, scenario analysis (red/green market projections).
A
Smart Alerts
Automated alerts for regime changes, breakout signals, stress threshold breaches, and significant market events. Mark read/unread.
W
Watchlist Heatmap
Visual heatmap of your watchlist showing ensemble scores, regime colors, Hurst values, and change percentages at a glance.
M
Sector Rotation Map
Relative strength of NIFTY sectors vs benchmark. Identifies outperforming, underperforming, and inline sectors for rotation strategies.
C
Market Calendar
Upcoming market events — holidays, expiry dates, RBI policy, earnings seasons, and IPO listings that may impact trading.

Free, no API key needed. Supports NIFTY stocks (RELIANCE.NS, TCS.NS, etc.), global indices (^NSEI, ^GSPC, ^DJI), crypto (BTC-USD, ETH-USD), and commodities (GC=F, CL=F, SI=F).

Explore Panels
Navigate the sidebar to view different analysis: Dashboard for overview, MFDFA/MFDCCA for deep fractal data, Breakout Scanner for trade signals, Smart Tools for health scores, regime predictions, and more.

Use AI Features
Generate market insights, chat with AI about your stock data, or create a full trade plan. Requires ANTHROPIC_API_KEY environment variable.
